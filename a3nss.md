## The AstroAI Asian Network Seminar Series (A<sup>3</sup>NSS)

The **AstroAI Asian Network ([A<sup>3</sup> Net](https://cd3.ipmu.jp/a3n/))** promotes education, research, and collaboration in artificial intelligence and machine learning (AI/ML) for astrophysics and cosmology, with a focus on building a strong regional network across Asia.

The **AstroAI Asian Network Seminar Series (A<sup>3</sup>NSS)** is a monthly online seminar series that connects A<sup>3</sup> Net members and highlights recent advances.

The seminar series is held **monthly** in an online format. Each session features a **30-minute talk**, followed by a **30-minute open discussion**, designed to encourage in-depth questions, cross-disciplinary exchange, and active participation from researchers at all career stages.

### Call for Abstracts

We invite A<sup>3</sup> Net members to submit abstracts. Researchers at all career stages are encouraged.
